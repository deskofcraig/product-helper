# Project kick-off template

Use this template at the start of every piece of work. Fill it in
through conversation with the project planner agent, or manually
before your kick-off meeting. The more completely it is filled in,
the more accurately the planner agent can generate your task list,
recommend which specialist agents to involve, and sequence your work.
